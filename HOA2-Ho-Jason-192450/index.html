About Me

Work

I started off as a non-technical founder for three startups I cofounded in my sophomore year of college. Afterwards, I decided to become technical and helped other startups with business and product while I was learning how to code. After learning how to code and build software, I then started working in software engineering. Currently, I am a software engineer at a venture studio called Kaya Founders.

Personal

When I have free time, I like to spend a lot of my time learning about startups - I read books, listen to podcasts, and watch talks from founders who have already established themselves as successful startup founders. I also enjoy listening to music and watching shows on Netflix.
